# KVRN Conversion Audit

## Funnel Analysis

### Homepage
- **Hero:** Full-viewport campaign image with copy and CTA. V3 conditional framework applied: CTA present at launch phase (pre-brand equity).
- **Scroll cue:** Subtle down arrow fades after 80px scroll. Back-to-top arrow appears after 80% viewport scroll. Reduces bounce from non-scrolling visitors.
- **Announcement bar:** 5 rotating messages. Complimentary shipping threshold, mailing list offer, processing time, new arrivals, secure checkout. Fades after scroll. Trust signals before page engagement.
- **Products:** 4 products across 2 collections. Grid layout. Color swatches visible without hover — reduces clicks to intent.
- **Phantom section:** Dark background differentiates the second collection visually — creates section identity.
- **Trust block:** 4 signals above the fold: secure checkout, store credit returns, processing speed, material quality. Addresses the top 4 conversion objections.

### Product Page
- **Currency:** Prices shown in customer's selected currency. Reduces friction for international visitors.
- **Stock badge:** "Sold out" at 0, "Few left" at 1–2. No badge otherwise. Honest urgency.
- **Size selector + guide link:** Adjacent to the selector. Reduces sizing hesitancy.
- **Sticky ATC:** Appears when main button scrolls out of view. Shows price + selected size. Reduces scroll-back friction.
- **Trust signals below ATC:** 4 points — secure checkout, store credit, processing time, material quality. Addresses post-click doubt.
- **Complete the Set:** Shown only when related product exists. No discount framing. AOV driver without cheapening.

### Cart Drawer
- **Shipping progress bar:** Real-time progress toward complimentary shipping. Smooth animation. Converts single-item carts to set purchases.
- **Store credit framing:** Replaces "free returns" with "store credit returns" — more accurate and premium.
- **Currency:** Cart totals shown in selected currency.

### Checkout
- **Multi-step:** Contact → Shipping → Payment. Email collected first (enables abandoned cart recovery).
- **Express checkout placeholder:** Apple Pay / Google Pay slots present. Activate by connecting Stripe Payment Request Button.
- **Order summary sticky:** Visible on desktop alongside the form. Reduces "what am I paying for?" anxiety.
- **Shipping cost shown:** Real-time calculation by country. No surprises at payment step.

## Conversion Psychology Implemented
1. Honest scarcity (stock badges) — builds trust through truthfulness
2. Progress mechanics (shipping bar) — drives AOV via loss aversion
3. Social proof via trust signals — reduces new-brand risk perception
4. Currency localization — removes "I need to convert this" mental friction
5. Sticky ATC — reduces effort required to complete purchase
6. Store credit over refunds — premium positioning, retains revenue
7. Size guide adjacent to selector — addresses top conversion blocker in fashion

## Recommendations (Not Yet Implemented)
- Reviews: add after 30+ genuine orders. Target 14-day post-delivery request.
- Abandoned cart email: activate at Stage 3 (100+ orders/month) per V3 blueprint.
- Wishlist: wire up backend storage when returning customer accounts are added.
- Product videos: fabric macro + hood demonstration videos on PDP improve conversion 15–25% in testing for premium apparel.
